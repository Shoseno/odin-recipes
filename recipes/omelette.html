Sho's Omelette Recipe!

On this webpage you will learn how to create a wonderful omelette in just 5 minutes!

Recipe Description

This 4 egg omelette is easy to make and requires very little time. it is the perfect quick and nutritious breakfast for anyone.

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • salt and pepper
  • Non sick pan
  • Small spatula or fork(fork may scratch pan)
  • bowl

Steps

  1. pre heat pan on medium heat
  2. break 4 eggs into a bowl
  3. whisk eggs in bowl until yolk and whites are one consistent colour
  4. add preffered amount of salt and pepper
  5. pour eggs into pan
  6. shake pan forward and back while whisking eggs in a figure 8 motion with spatula or fork
  7. when eggs are a runnyness of your liking: turn the heat off and peel egg off the edges of the pan
  8. fold far side of egg towards near side
  9. transfer omelette from pan to plate
